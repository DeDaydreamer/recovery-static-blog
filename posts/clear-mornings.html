Clear Mornings

15 January 2025

Starting mornings calmly sets the tone for the day. I wake with intention, letting light spill in slowly before any screens or noise. A glass of water, a deep breath, and a moment to notice how I feel are the first steps.

After water comes a short walk, just enough to stretch and greet the air. I set a simple intention—often only a word—to guide my focus. The aim is softness, not speed.

Reducing noise keeps the day gentle. Music stays low, notifications stay silent, and I choose tasks that match the calm. Clear mornings invite softer days.
