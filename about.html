About

The Daydreamverse is a personal writing space for recovery, understanding, and quiet reflections. It collects gentle notes, observations, and reminders that help keep healing steady and honest.

Everything here is written slowly, with room for nuance, pauses, and the softer parts of change. This is where I keep track of the small wins, the questions that linger, and the practices that help me return to myself.
