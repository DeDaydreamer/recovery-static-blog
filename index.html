Understanding recovery

Everything I publish here is meant to bring value to You - my reader. The only agenda I have is to help you understand addiction. I think that in today therapies, there is not enough knowledge about how the human brain work and what addiction is from science - neurobiology, neurochemistry, human anatomy - perspective.

This is my personal opinion and I don't mean to force it on anybody. I am not a scientist, but I am a junkie who managed to survive and get clean. This blog documents what helped me, and if it helps only one person - it's worth the effort to build and maintain it.

Please consider a donation. Not for me. For the Daydream Foundation. Let's help bring the understanding to the prevention, recovery and harm control of addiction.
